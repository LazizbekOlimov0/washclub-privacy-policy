Wash Club - Account Deletion Request

Wash Club respects your privacy and gives you the ability to request deletion of your account and associated personal data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the Wash Club application.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.

If you cannot access the application, you may request account deletion by contacting our support team.

Email: bobur3924@gmail.com

What data will be deleted?

  • User profile information
  • Telegram account association
  • Saved personal information
  • Booking history associated with your account (unless retention is required by law)

Data retention

After your deletion request is confirmed, your account and associated personal data will generally be permanently deleted within 30 days, unless we are legally required to retain certain information.
